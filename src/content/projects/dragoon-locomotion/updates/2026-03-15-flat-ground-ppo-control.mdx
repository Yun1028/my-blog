## Goal of This Stage

The purpose of this update was to teach the robot stable and basic motion skills on flat terrain before moving on to harder environments.

The key targets were:

- moving forward
- rotating on command
- stopping and staying still when needed

These basic motion behaviors were necessary before attempting rough terrain locomotion or more complex navigation tasks.

---

## Training Process

At the beginning of this stage, the training results were unstable and did not match the intended motion behavior.

The robot did not immediately learn proper forward motion, rotation, or stopping.
After repeated reward adjustment and training analysis, the policy gradually became more aligned with the target behavior.
